# 🚲 Cyclistic Bike Share Analysis | Google Data Analytics Capstone

This capstone project analyzes Cyclistic bike-share usage patterns using BigQuery SQL and Tableau Public to identify how casual riders and annual members use the service differently, with the aim of supporting membership growth strategies.

Ask, Prepare, Process, Analyze, Share, and Act

---

## Ask

### Business Task

Cyclistic’s marketing team wants to increase annual memberships.

**How do annual members and casual riders use Cyclistic bikes differently?**

---

## Prepare

Cyclistic offers:
- single-ride passes  
- day passes  
- annual memberships  

Riders:
- Casual riders  
- Members  

---

## Process

- Combined 12 datasets  
- Removed invalid rides  
- Created:
  - ride_length  
  - day_of_week  
  - month  
  - ride_hour

## Share

The dashboards show:

- ride frequency  
- duration patterns  
- weekday vs weekend trends  
- seasonal usage  
- peak hours  
- bike preferences  

---

## Key Findings

- Members ride more frequently  
- Casual riders ride longer  
- Members ride weekdays  
- Casual riders ride weekends  

---

## Act

### Recommendations

1. Weekend membership campaigns  
2. Seasonal promotions  
3. Cost-saving messaging  
4. Time-based marketing  

---

## Conclusion

Members = routine, short trips  
Casual riders = longer, leisure trips
